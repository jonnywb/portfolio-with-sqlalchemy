About Me

For the last few years I've been working as a freelance Musician, working for two of the oldest cruise companies based in the UK, Cunard and P&O. As a trombonist, I was a member of the ballroom and latin dance band for two years, until transitioning to the theatre. I now work as a member of the Royal Court theatre band on all three of Cunard's ships including the last remaining ocean liner in the world - Queen Mary 2.

Growing up, I learned brass instruments from an early age, and went on to study at the Royal Northern College of Music in Manchester, UK. Although health reasons forced me to eventually drop out, I still thoroughly enjoyed my time at the College and learned a huge amount from some of the most brilliant musicians in the world.

A few years later, I ended up studying a course at Manchester's School of Sound Recording (now Spirit Studios). I learned a huge amount about engineering sound in Recording Studios and for Television and Film.

I've used many of the skills I've learned to upload music, sound effects and video to HitRECord - an online production company ran by Joseph Gordon Levitt, and have featured in some amazing work including an LG Television Advert, a Youtube Original series and also a Netflix promotional video.

In my spare time, I love photography, football #Upthetics #wafc, gaming and television/film. You can see my photography by visiting the instagram link at the bottom of the page.
